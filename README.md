## 🌟 The Story

In the Mahabharata, when Arjuna stood confused and lost on the battlefield of Kurukshetra — Krishna spoke.

Today, when modern Indians face their own battles — confusion, guilt, broken relationships, moral dilemmas, career crossroads — **Madhav.ai speaks.**

Just as Krishna addressed Arjuna as **"Parth"** —
Madhav.ai addresses YOU as **Parth.**

---

## 🎯 What is Madhav.ai?

Madhav.ai is **NOT** a religious app.

It is a **Dharma-based ethical decision guidance system** — built on the timeless wisdom of:

- 📖 **Bhagavad Gita** — Krishna's direct teachings
- ⚔️ **Mahabharata** — Stories of Dharma vs Adharma
- 🌺 **Ramayana** — Righteousness in action
- 🦚 **Sri Krishna's Life** — From Gokul to Kurukshetra

**Ask your real-life dilemmas. Get ancient wisdom.**

- 🤖 **AI powered by Google Gemini** — Free, fast, intelligent
- 🌐 **Bilingual** — Reply in English or Hindi based on your question
- 🔐 **User Authentication** — Signup, Login, Guest mode
- 💬 **Real-time Chat** — Smooth, animated chat interface
- 🎨 **Beautiful UI** — Dark spiritual theme with Sanskrit aesthetics
- 📱 **Mobile Responsive** — Works on all devices
